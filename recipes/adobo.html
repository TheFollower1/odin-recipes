The following image shows the national dish of the philippines. It is marinated chicken which is soaked in a sauce containing 4 main ingredients. The ingredients are soy sauce, vinegar, whole black pepper corn, and bay leaves.

Some people will add their own twists to the recipe, like brown sugar that adds a level of sweetness to the already aromatic and zesty flavor.

Ingredients

  • Chicken Breasts
  • Soy Sauce
  • Vinegar
  • Whole Black Peppercorns
  • Bay Leaves

Steps

  1. Combine all ingredients to make sauce
  2. Coat and refrigerate chicken with marinade sauce for at least one hour.
  3. Cook chicken on low to medium heat for about 30 minutes until chicken reaches an internal temperature of 165*
