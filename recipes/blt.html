BLT Sandwich

The following image shows the amazing and delicious yet simple american BLT. The american BLT showcases simplicity and flavor all in one sandwich. You can't go wrong with this dish. With 4 ingredients and 10 minutes, you're on your way to a meal worth having everyday.

The classic american BLT can be eaten at your leisure everyday, with any sauce of your liking to amplify the already delicious meal. It's lightweight, pack full of flavor and considerably very healthy compared to your counterpart like the hamburger.

Ingredients

  • Sliced Bread
  • Romaine Lettuce
  • Slice Tomatoes
  • Uncured Bacon Slices

Steps

  1. Heat oil in a medium size pan and cook bacon slices until crispy.
  2. Heat bread up in rendered fat making toasted bread.
  3. Create sandwich with bread, lettuce, tomato and bacon.
