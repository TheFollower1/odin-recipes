Fried Oreos

The following image shows a great festival go to meal. It is the Fried Oreos, it is sweet and fluffy. Although it contains a mini heart attack if you eat too much, it is definitely a fan favorite.

While eating the Fried Oreo is great by itself, adding extra toppings such as powdered sugar and caramel or chocolate syrup will enhance both the taste and experience.

Ingredients

  • Oreos store bought
  • Funnel Cake Mix
  • Oil (Either vegetable or canola oil)

Steps

  1. Heat oil in a large cooking pan until you reach 400*.
  2. Mix funnel cake batter with one part water and one part batter.
  3. Dip Oreo in batter and cook in oil until golden brown.
